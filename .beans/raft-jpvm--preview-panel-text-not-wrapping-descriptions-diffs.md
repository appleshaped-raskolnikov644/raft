Text inside the PR preview panel does not wrap, making long lines barely readable. Affects PR descriptions, code diffs, and AI comments/explanations. Descriptions and AI comments must soft-wrap at the panel boundary. This is a major usability issue since the preview panel is the primary way to understand what a PR does.
